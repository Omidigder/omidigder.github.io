About Me

I am a PhD researcher specializing in hydrological modeling, uncertainty analysis, and data assimilation. My research focuses on understanding how errors in precipitation, model structure, and parameters propagate through hydrological systems and affect streamflow prediction and forecasting skill.

I have extensive experience working with physically based and conceptual hydrological models, satellite-based observations, and advanced statistical and machine learning techniques.

Research Interests

  • Hydrological and hydrometeorological modeling
  • Uncertainty and sensitivity analysis (Sobol, ANOVA, Information Decomposition)
  • Data assimilation (EnKF, Particle Filter, Asynchronous EnKF)
  • Satellite-based precipitation and soil moisture
  • Streamflow forecasting and error propagation
  • Snow-dominated and cold-region hydrology
